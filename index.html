About Double Blind News

Double Blind is created by the VirtualSushi team and is currently accessible via a desktop and a mobile site and via an Android app while the iOS app is under way.

Double Blind

We constantly scan all tweets published by a panel of about 2000 multidisciplinary techies, mostly software developers from all over the world. Every 15 minutes on the 15th minute, we filter out the 5 most important/popular entries and publish them here and on our android app (iOS is underway).
